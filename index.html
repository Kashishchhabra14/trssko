Kashish Chhabra

Founder & Managing Director

A distinguished legal professional and technologist, Kashish Chhabra serves as the visionary lead for TRSSKO Enterprises. Registered as an Advocate with the Bar Council of Uttar Pradesh, he infuses the company’s global operations with a rigorous framework of legal compliance and ethical transparency.

Complementing his legal acumen, his background as a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) graduate allows him to leverage advanced digital infrastructure to streamline international supply chains and ensure technical precision in export logistics.

Since founding the enterprise in early 2021, he has successfully scaled TRSSKO into a multi-sectoral powerhouse—spanning FMCG, Pharmaceuticals, and Apparel—while securing critical intellectual property and corporate registrations.
